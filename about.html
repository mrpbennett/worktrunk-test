About Us

Welcome to MyApp — where we turn coffee into code and bugs into features (sometimes).

Founded in 2024 by a group of developers who couldn't decide on a restaurant, we specialize in making things that do stuff. Our mission is to solve problems you didn't know you had, while using too many parentheses.

When we're not Googling our own errors, we enjoy long walks on the beach and debugging CSS at 2am. Our favorite quote: "It works on my machine" — Anonymous Developer
